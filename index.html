Chess Techniques

Mastering chess techniques is essential for strategic gameplay. Understanding various tactical maneuvers can give you a significant advantage over your opponents. Here are some key techniques:

  • Fork: Attack two or more pieces simultaneously.
  • Pin: Immobilize an opponent's piece.
  • Skewer: Attack two pieces in a line, forcing the opponent to move the more valuable piece.
  • Discovered Attack: Unleash an attack when a piece moves, revealing the attack of another piece.
